George Mason Honors College RTOC

During the Fall semester 2013, I was employed by the George Mason University Honors College as a part of the Research, Technology, and Online Community (RTOC) initiative with the goal of developing online interactive tools to support first year students engaging in complex research processes.

Pursuant with my position, I led the development of a project to assist students with the research process. Specifically, the interactive web service allows students to anonymously submit their research questions for crowd-sourced community peer-review. The academic and critical conversation is facilitated by a rating and commenting system. A user-logging system adds accountability to the student discussion, and allows faculty and mentors to moderate the service.
